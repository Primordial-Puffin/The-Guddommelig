# The Guddommelig Lexicon

A reference guide to the language, concepts, and terminology of Worylia.

---

## A

### Anima
*noun*
The measure of one's awakened power, grown through Saythuuring. 
Each awakening increases anima by one.

---

## S

### Saythuuring
*noun*
A moment of terrible revelation that awakens a being to hidden truths 
of the universe, granting a permanent connection to a corresponding leyline 
at the cost of mental health.

---

## G

### Guddommelig
*noun*
The leyline of the gods, serving as both conduit for divine power and 
repository of cosmic knowledge. Access is typically reserved for deities.
